An Introduction to some Groups I Listen To

I would like to introduce you to some girl groups that I listen to, most of which are kpop. These groups are Dreamcatcher, Babymetal, Gfriend, and EXID. Dreamcatcher is considered in the mixture of kpop but they also experiment with rock and nu-metal. Babymetal is the odd one out, since they are a Japanese girl group that sings and dances to metal as if it were pop music. Gfriend and Itzy are both strictly kpop, but Gfriend leans into ballads occasionally. They have more elegant and peppy sounding music, while Itzy is moreso “confidence” and girl-crush-y.
