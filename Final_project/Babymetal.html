A Group that Combines Genres...but a little differently

Babymetal

Babymetal is a three member girl group under Amuse entertainment. They consist of Su-metal, Moametal, and Momometal. Momometal is a recent addition to the group, since the group used to have Yuimetal, whom left years ago. Babymetal continued as a two member girl group up until recently. They became incredibly popular in the mid 2010’s for songs like “Gimme Chocolate!!”, “Megitsune,” and “Karate.” They have recently released the concept album “The Other One.” Below is a recent music video for the song “Light and Darkness” off that album. I will also be seeing Babymetal in concert along with a band called Dethlok in September!
