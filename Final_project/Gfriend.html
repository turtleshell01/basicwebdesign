Ballads in a Kpop Group?

Gfriend

Gfriend was a six member girl group under HYBE entertainment. They consisted of Sowon, Eunha, Yerin, SinB, Yuju, and Umji. They were formed in 2015 but abruptly disbanded in 2021. The songs they would release would often bounce back and forth from a ballad pop song to a peppy pop song. Their debut song was titled “Glass Bead” while their last song released was “Mago” in November of 2020. This is a group that I wish I had seen in concert but unfortunately, they never toured anywhere outside of East Asia. In 2022, three of the members came together to form the group “Viviz” which is currently active.
