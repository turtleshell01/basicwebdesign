Itzy

The most recent group to debut out of all the ones discussed is Itzy, a five member group under JYP entertainment. Members consist of Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. They debuted in February 2019 with the song “Dalla Dalla” which has essentially set the theme of most of their songs up until today. The theme is about being confident and true to yourself. Common lyrics themes are phrases such as “I love myself” and “I won’t change for anyone else.” This is also another group that I saw back in concert back in November and was one of the most fun concerts I have ever been to!
