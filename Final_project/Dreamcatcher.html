A Group that Combines Genres

Dreamcatcher

Dreamcatcher is a seven member girl group under Happyface entertainment. They consist of Jiu, SuA, Siyeon, Handong, Yoohyeon, Dami, and Gahyeon. They debuted on January 13th, 2017 with the song “Chase Me.” Coincidentally, January 13th is a Friday the 13th which coincides with the creepy concept of the song. They recently have released the song “Reason” to commemorate their sixth anniversary. I actually saw them in concert in Atlanta back in February and I went to their meet and greet! One of my favorite songs is the title track from their EP “Apocalypse: Follow Us” titled “Vision” which you can listen to below.
